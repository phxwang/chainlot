CryptoLotto6.org is an Ethereum based lottery. Everyone can purchase tickets with ETH and get CLT (CryptoLottoToken) through purchasement. One new jackpot pool will be initialized in every 50000 block. 10% of the pool will go to CLT. The Jackpot number of every pool will be calculated based on all the tickets number in the pool, the hash of the drawing block and the timestamp of the drawing block. All the prize will be returned as CryptoLottoCoin(CLC).

Enter 6 numbers（1-70 for the first 5 numbers, 1-25 for the sixth number）
